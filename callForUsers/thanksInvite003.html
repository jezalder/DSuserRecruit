Participate in our online study to help us improve DataSearch

Thanks for agreeing to help us test DataSearch. We'd now like to invite you to participate in an online study.

The study consists of a small set of questions about you and research data, a handful of tasks using the tool, and some further closing questions about your experience.

The session will be done using UserZoom, and requires that you install a secure plug-in in order for us to see what you do on the site. The plugin will be automatically removed once the study is complete.

In total this should take no more than 20 minutes to complete. Your answers will be confidential and won't be seen by anyone except the people working on the project.

Upon successful completion of the study, you will receive a $25 (or equivalent) Amazon gift voucher (unless your name is Bojhan).

If you have any questions or comments about the study, please feel free to contact us at datasearch@elsevier.com.
